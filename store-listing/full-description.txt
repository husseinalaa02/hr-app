AFAQ ALFIKER HR Management System

The official HR app for AFAQ ALFIKER employees — everything you need to manage your work life in one place.

KEY FEATURES

📍 Attendance & Check-In
Clock in and out directly from your phone. Location-based geofencing ensures check-ins happen only from the office. View your weekly attendance history at a glance.

🗓 Leave Requests
Submit leave requests in seconds — annual leave, sick leave, emergency leave and more. Track approval status in real time.

📋 Day Requests
Request remote work, early departure, or late arrival directly from the app.

💰 Payslips & Payroll
Access your monthly payslips securely anytime. View salary breakdowns, deductions, and net pay.

🧾 Expense Claims
Submit and track expense reimbursement requests with ease.

⭐ Appraisals
View your performance appraisals and ratings submitted by your manager.

🔔 Real-Time Notifications
Get instant notifications for approvals, rejections, payroll releases, and announcements.

👥 Employee Directory
Browse the company directory, view team members' profiles, departments, and contact information.

📊 Reports (Managers & HR)
HR managers and admins can access detailed reports on attendance, leaves, expenses, and headcount.

- Data encrypted in transit and at rest
